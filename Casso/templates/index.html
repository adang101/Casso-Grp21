CASSO

Social Media Art Marketplace

CASSO is a project created by the four students of Group 21 of CMPE 133 at San Jose State University.

With deadlines to meet, grades to attain, and bottomless creativity, Alvin Dang, Binh Vu, Jun Jie Lai, and Lam Vang crafted an ever evolving digital gallery. Enabling artists around the world to abandon the limits of physical constraints as they turn a new leaf and partake in a vibrant atmosphere of creativity.

However, the four students didn't stop there. Drawing on their own experiences, they made CASSO more than just a gallery. They turned it into a commission based marketplace. Here at CASSO, artists can sell their works and services to regular users whilst also being able to connect with other artists.
